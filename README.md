# Invoice-Profit-Margin-Report
This project consists in the calculation of the profit margin for each supplier invoice by comparing the cost of purchased products with their corresponding sales. It provides a clear view of the profit at the invoice level, allowing for accurate analysis of invoice-level margins. 

To carry out this exercise, the tables Costs, Invoice_Detail, Prices, Products, Suppliers, and Sales were created in order to relate them and perform a query that compares sales and costs, thereby obtaining the profit margin in both monetary and percentage terms.

Calculating the profit margin is essential for understanding the financial performance of each invoice. It allows businesses to identify which products or suppliers are most profitable, make informed pricing decisions, and ensure sustainable operations.
